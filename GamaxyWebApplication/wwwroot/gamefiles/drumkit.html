Features

  • Click on drums or use keyboard keys to play sounds
  • Use the Beat Maker to create and play drum patterns
  • Record your performances and play them back
  • Adjust volume using the slider at the bottom

Tips for Beginners

  • Start with a simple beat: kick (space) on 1 and 3, snare (F) on 2 and 4
  • Add hi-hat (E) on every beat to keep time
  • Use the Beat Maker to learn common drum patterns
  • Practice with the pre-made patterns before creating your own
